My name is Sonia Portaluppi. I study Public Relations with a specialization in Communication Studies at the University of Florida.

Before chosing public relations as a career path, I dreamt of being a journalist given my passion for writing. It wasn't until I researched the creative aspect of business that I discovered public relations.

I found this major to be the perfect blend of two roles I could see myself fulfilling: communicator and businesswoman.

Although the internships I partook in were not specifically public relations internships, the discipline was used on a daily basis both strategically and creatively. As a future public relations practioner, my mentors have assured me that it is essential to be exposed to every aspect that goes into a public relations strategy.

These internships focused on marketing, social resposibility, fashion, luxury products, social media management and content creation. All of these are integral skills to anyone in the public relations field.
